What is Growth Mindset?

People who believe their skills and talents can be developed through hard work, good strategies and input from others, have growth mindset. Individuals with this mindset tend to achieve more than those with a more fixed mindset who believe their talents are innate, or skills they are born with. This is because people with growth mindset spend more time learning and developing than those with a fixed mindset.

Why is it relevant?

Everyone is actually a mixture of both fixed and growth mindsets that evolves with experience, a ‘pure’ growth mindset doesn’t exist. This is relevant in our learning as we have to acknowledge the things we are already good at and also the things we’re not so good at, in order to receive the benefits we’re after. That way we can build on these foundations and learn to shift our weaknesses into a more positive direction.
